WELCOME TO INSIGHTLY!

Insightly is a tool that is used to efficiently analyze research papers, without needing to read through the entire paper. It streamlines easy access to published papers and significantly reduces researching time. This platform allows the user to seamlessly get the essence of the essay and its key points, thereby saving time and effort. Click below to get started!

How does it work?

Insightly is a web platform that:

  • Summarize the research papers
  • Gets tagged topic headers for the paper
  • Has Q&A to get more info on research topics
  • Shows similar paper recommendations
  • Safely storing research papers in the database for ease of access
